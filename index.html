ℹ Instructions

Welcome, My Daily Planner is a 24 hour time scheduling application that saves user input to localStorage.

Each timeblock is color coded to indicate the time of day:

  • ▶ 🟢 Indicates the time left in the day
  • ▶ 🔴 Indicates current time
  • ▶ ⚪ Indicates that the time has passed

Any button with 💾 will save your entry

Any button with 🗑️ will delete your entry.

Enjoy!
